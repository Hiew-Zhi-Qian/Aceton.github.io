Teacher Jet Wong

Teacher Jet Wong has a strong passion for teaching mathematics. While studied in university, he started to provide private teaching. From there, he discovered his passion for teaching and came to understand the impact of helping students who are struggling in mathematics and find joy in learning the subject.

Teacher Jet specialise in Math & Add Math subject and he helps student (Form 1-5) to score well through Proven Math Learning Methodology.

How Do We Teach?

  1. LDM Method - Learn the WHY not just the WHAT so that students will know have to implement in their daily life, and not only memories the formula.
  2. Small-Steps Approach - Our materials are organized into different levels each with own themes so that it is fun to follow in steps.
  3. We provide a full revision plan to make sure students fully understand the importance of each chapter.
  4. We focus on important topics which may come out during the exam and provide more than 2000 questions compilling from trial and past exam.
  5. We provide simplified techniques for students to understand the foundation and concept so that they can solve examination questions.
  6. We continually update on students progress with parents.
